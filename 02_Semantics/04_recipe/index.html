Simple Pasta Recipe

A quick and easy homemade pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Boil water and add salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8–10 minutes.
  3. Heat oil and add garlic.
  4. Add tomato sauce and mix.
  5. Combine pasta with sauce and serve.

Tips

You can add vegetables or cheese for extra flavor.
